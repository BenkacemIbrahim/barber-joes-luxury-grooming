Cancellation Policy

Please review our cancellation and rescheduling policies to ensure a smooth experience.

Last updated: January 1, 2024

Cancellation Notice

We understand that sometimes plans change. To ensure we can accommodate all our clients and maintain our high level of service, we require:

24-hour advance notice for all cancellations and rescheduling requests.

This policy allows us to offer your appointment time to other clients who may be waiting for an opening.

How to Cancel or Reschedule

You can cancel or reschedule your appointment through any of the following methods:

  • Call us directly at (123) 456-7890
  • Email us at appointments@barberjoes.com
  • Use our online booking system (if available)
  • Visit us in person at 123 Main Street

Please have your appointment details ready when contacting us.

Late Cancellations

Cancellations made with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to:

  • A cancellation fee of 50% of the service cost
  • Difficulty securing future preferred appointment times
  • Potential requirement for advance payment for future bookings

Note: We understand that emergencies happen. Please contact us to discuss your situation, and we'll work with you when possible.

No-Show Policy

If you fail to show up for your appointment without prior notice:

  • You will be charged the full service fee
  • Future appointments may require advance payment
  • Repeated no-shows may result in booking restrictions

We reserve the right to refuse service to clients with a pattern of no-shows.

Late Arrivals

We strive to stay on schedule for all our clients. If you arrive late:

  • Up to 15 minutes late: We'll do our best to accommodate you with minimal impact to your service
  • 15-30 minutes late: Your service time may be shortened to avoid delays for other clients
  • More than 30 minutes late: Your appointment may need to be rescheduled

Weather and Emergency Situations

In case of severe weather, natural disasters, or other emergency situations that prevent you from keeping your appointment, we will waive our standard cancellation policy. Please contact us as soon as it's safe to do so to reschedule your appointment.

Rescheduling

We're happy to help you reschedule your appointment. Please note:

  • Rescheduling requests must follow the same 24-hour notice policy
  • We'll do our best to accommodate your preferred new time
  • Popular time slots may have limited availability
  • Multiple rescheduling requests may be subject to additional policies
